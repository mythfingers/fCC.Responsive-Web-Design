FEISAR proudly unveils its all-new FX400 craft for the FX400 Anti-Gravity Racing League, setting a new benchmark in agility, power, and control. After months of rigorous development and cutting-edge engineering, the FX400 is poised to dominate the season. Whether it's tight corners or blistering straightaways, this craft pushes the boundaries of performance to deliver unrivaled speed and stability.

Razor-Sharp Agility

Engineered with unparalleled handling precision, the FX400 thrives where other crafts struggle. Our latest adaptive steering technology ensures supreme control through high-G corners and chicanes, giving FEISAR pilots the confidence to shave seconds off lap times.

Aerodynamic Precision

The FX400 boasts a redesigned aerodynamic profile, honed through extensive wind-tunnel testing. Its lower drag coefficient allows for superior top speed on long straights while maintaining critical downforce for tight track sections. This balance between speed and grip ensures maximum performance, no matter the circuit.

Next-Gen Energy Management

FEISAR’s advanced energy recovery systems push efficiency to new heights. The FX400 recycles kinetic energy more effectively than any competitor, providing pilots with an enhanced turbo boost for overtakes and critical defensive maneuvers. When every millisecond counts, FEISAR’s innovation makes all the difference.
